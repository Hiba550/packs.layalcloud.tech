Thermal Expansion

Industrial machinery and energy systems for automated processing and power generation

Forge Tech by CoFH Team

Description

Thermal Expansion is a comprehensive technology mod that adds industrial machinery, energy systems, and automation to Minecraft. It's part of the Thermal series and provides a balanced progression from simple machines to advanced automated systems.

Energy System

  • Redstone Flux (RF) energy system
  • Steam dynamos and thermal generators
  • Energy cells for power storage
  • Leadstone to Resonant tier progression
  • Efficient energy transfer with conduits
  • Renewable energy options

Industrial Machines

  • Pulverizer for ore doubling
  • Redstone furnace for efficient smelting
  • Induction smelter for alloy creation
  • Fluid transposer for liquid handling
  • Magma crucible for melting items
  • Centrifugal separator for resource processing

Automation Features

  • Itemducts for item transportation
  • Fluiducts for liquid transport
  • Servo and retriever upgrades
  • Pneumatic servos for smart filtering
  • Redstone control integration
  • Machine augments for customization

Advanced Technology

  • Tesseract for wireless transport
  • Portable tanks and strongboxes
  • Thermal mediator for environment control
  • Aqueous accumulator for infinite water
  • Igneous extruder for cobblestone generation
