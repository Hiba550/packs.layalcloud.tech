Waystones

Teleportation network using ancient waystones for fast travel across your world

Fabric/Forge Travel by BlayTheNinth

Description

Waystones adds a fast travel system to Minecraft through the use of ancient waystones that can be activated and used for teleportation. Perfect for large worlds and multiplayer servers where walking long distances becomes tedious.

Core Features

  • Craftable and naturally spawning waystones
  • Right-click activation system
  • Teleportation between discovered waystones
  • Customizable waystone names
  • Global and local waystone networks
  • Beautiful, immersive waystone designs

Travel Options

  • Waystone-to-waystone teleportation
  • Warp scrolls for portable teleportation
  • Return scrolls to get back home
  • Bound scrolls linked to specific waystones
  • Warp stones for reusable travel
  • Configurable teleportation costs

World Integration

  • Natural generation in villages and structures
  • Compatible with dimension mods
  • Multiplayer-friendly with shared networks
  • Configurable generation rates
  • Permission system for server administration

Quality of Life

  • Essential for large modded worlds
  • Reduces tedious travel time
  • Encourages exploration
  • Perfect for multiplayer cooperation
  • Balanced with configurable costs
