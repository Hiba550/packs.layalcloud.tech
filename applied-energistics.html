Description

Applied Energistics 2 (AE2) is one of the most sophisticated technology mods for Minecraft, providing an advanced digital storage and automation system that revolutionizes how you manage items and automate crafting in large-scale builds.

Digital Storage System

  • Store millions of items in compact drives
  • Digital item access through terminals
  • Wireless access points and tablets
  • Automatic item sorting and organization
  • Fuzzy and precise item matching
  • Crafting pattern storage and automation

Automation Features

  • Complex multi-step recipe automation
  • Export/import buses for machine integration
