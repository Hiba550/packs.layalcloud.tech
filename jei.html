Just Enough Items (JEI)

Recipe viewer and item management system for easier crafting

Forge Utility by mezz

Description

Just Enough Items (JEI) is the most popular recipe viewing mod for Minecraft Forge. It provides an intuitive interface to view crafting recipes, uses for items, and manage your inventory more efficiently.

Features

  • Complete recipe browser for all items
  • Search functionality with advanced filters
  • Recipe transfer directly to crafting tables
  • Item usage and crafting information
  • Mod integration for custom recipes
  • Bookmarks system for favorite items
  • Creative mode item spawning

Quality of Life

  • Never forget crafting recipes again
  • Discover new items and their uses
  • Efficient inventory management
  • Perfect for modded gameplay

Mod Support

  • Works with 1000+ mods
  • Automatic recipe detection
  • Custom recipe category support
