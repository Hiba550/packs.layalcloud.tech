About Occultism

Occultism is an innovative magic mod that introduces otherworldly powers through rituals, demon summoning, and spirit-based automation. Unlike traditional tech mods, Occultism provides automation through supernatural means, allowing players to summon and bind spirits to perform tasks from mining to item transportation.

Key Features

  • Spirit Familiars: Summon helpful spirits that can automate resource gathering and item transportation
  • Dimensional Storage: Access virtually unlimited storage through otherworldly dimensions
  • Magical Mining: Summon mining spirits to automatically extract ores from specific chunks
  • Ritual Magic: Perform complex rituals using chalk drawings, candles, and sacrifices
  • Demon Summoning: Bind powerful demons to perform specialized tasks
  • Magical Transportation: Create interdimensional item networks without pipes or cables
  • Magical Tools: Craft specialized tools infused with supernatural properties
  • Dictionary of Spirits: In-game guide to all summonable entities and rituals

Getting Started

Begin your journey into the arcane by crafting a Book of Binding and finding Otherworld Sapling trees. These special trees provide materials for your first rituals. Progress through the Dictionary of Spirits to unlock more powerful summons and automation possibilities.

Compatibility

Occultism works well with most magic and tech mods, including Botania, Blood Magic, and Create. The mod provides integration with popular automation mods, allowing its spiritual transportation systems to connect with traditional pipes and machines.
