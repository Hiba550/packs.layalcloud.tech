Nature's Compass

Essential exploration tool that helps locate any biome quickly

Fabric/Forge Utility by Chaosyr

Description

Nature's Compass is an essential exploration mod that adds a magical compass capable of locating any biome in your Minecraft world. Whether you're searching for a specific biome for resources, building, or just exploration, this compass will point you in the right direction, saving hours of wandering.

Features

  • Locate any biome instantly with a magical compass
  • Easy-to-use interface with biome search and selection
  • Distance indication to target biome
  • Works with all vanilla and modded biomes
  • Configurable search radius and performance settings
  • Waypoint-style navigation to found biomes
  • Teleportation commands for creative mode
  • Biome information and statistics

How to Use

  • Craft the Nature's Compass with standard materials
  • Right-click to open the biome selection interface
  • Choose your desired biome from the comprehensive list
  • Follow the compass needle to reach your destination
  • Distance and coordinates are displayed for precision
  • Reset or change target biomes at any time

Biome Support

  • All vanilla Minecraft biomes
  • Modded biomes from popular mods
  • Nether and End dimension biomes
  • Custom biomes from world generation mods
  • Biome variants and sub-types
  • Rare and hard-to-find biomes

Configuration Options

  • Adjustable search radius for performance
  • Biome blacklist for excluding certain biomes
  • Creative mode teleportation toggle
  • Interface customization options
  • Distance units and display preferences
