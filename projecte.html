ProjectE

Equivalent Exchange & Matter Transmutation

Forge Magic by sinkillerj

Description

ProjectE brings the power of equivalent exchange to Minecraft, allowing you to transmute matter, convert energy, and create valuable resources from common materials. Master the art of alchemy with the philosopher's stone and energy systems.

Core Items

  • Philosopher's Stone for transmutation
  • Energy Condenser for matter creation
  • Transmutation Table for conversions
  • Energy Collector for EMC generation
  • Klein Stars for energy storage
  • Alchemical Chest with infinite capacity
  • Destruction Catalyst for mining
  • Hyperkinetic Lens for energy focus

Transmutation System

  • EMC Values: Every item has energy value
  • Matter Exchange: Convert items to energy and back
  • Equivalent Value: Trade equal energy amounts
  • Energy Storage: Klein Stars hold massive EMC
  • Automated Collection: Passive EMC generation
  • Watch of Flowing Time: Accelerate processes
  • Ring of Arcana: Magical effects and abilities
  • Condenser Networks: Automated transmutation
