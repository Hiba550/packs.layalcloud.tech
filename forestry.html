Forestry

Advanced farming and automation legacy

Forge Farming by SirSengir

Description

Forestry is one of the cornerstone mods of the modded Minecraft community, introducing advanced beekeeping, tree breeding, automated farming, and renewable energy systems. This classic mod has shaped the landscape of modded Minecraft for over a decade.

Beekeeping System

  • Advanced bee breeding with genetics
  • Dozens of bee species to discover
  • Apiaries for bee management
  • Honey, wax, and special resource production
  • Bee trait improvement through selective breeding
  • Climate and biome considerations

Tree Breeding

  • Genetic system for tree improvement
  • Custom tree species creation
  • Fruit tree cultivation
  • Automated forestry operations
  • Sapling analysis and breeding

Automation & Machinery

  • Automatic Farm for crop automation
  • Arboretum for tree farming
  • Peat Bog for fuel production
  • Various engines for power generation
  • Carpenter for advanced crafting
  • Centrifuge for resource processing

Additional Features

  • Mail system for multiplayer servers
  • Specialized backpacks for organization
  • Ethanol and biogas production
  • Circuit boards and bronze tools
  • Integration with many other mods
