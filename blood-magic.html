Blood Magic

Ritualistic Magic & Life Essence

Forge Magic by WayofTime

Description

Blood Magic introduces a dark and powerful magic system based on life essence, blood altars, and ritualistic practices. Master the arcane arts of sacrifice, transmutation, and demon summoning to unlock incredible magical powers.

Core Features

  • Blood Altar system for essence generation
  • Complex ritual magic with multiblock structures
  • Living armor that grows with experience
  • Demon summoning and binding
  • Alchemical arrays for transmutation
  • Sigil magic for powerful effects
  • Soul network for essence sharing
  • Blood orbs and tier progression

Magic Systems

  • Life Essence: The fundamental power source
  • Ritual Magic: Large-scale magical workings
  • Alchemy: Transmutation and potion brewing
  • Demon Magic: Summon and bind demons
  • Living Armor: Self-improving magical protection
  • Sigils: Portable magical tools
