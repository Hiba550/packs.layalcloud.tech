Farmer's Delight

Enhanced cooking and farming systems with new crops, cooking mechanics, and food recipes

Fabric/Forge Farming by vectorwing

Description

Farmer's Delight is a comprehensive mod that revolutionizes Minecraft's cooking and farming experience. It adds realistic cooking mechanics, new crops, advanced farming tools, and delicious recipes that make food preparation an engaging gameplay element.

Features

  • Advanced cooking mechanics with stoves, pots, and cutting boards
  • New crops including tomatoes, onions, cabbage, and rice
  • 100+ new food recipes and cooking combinations
  • Realistic cooking tools and utensils
  • Nutritional system with balanced meals
  • Composting and organic farming mechanics
  • Beautiful food presentation and serving dishes
  • Compatible with other farming mods

Cooking System

  • Multi-step cooking processes with realistic preparation
  • Cutting boards for ingredient preparation
  • Cooking pots for stews, soups, and complex dishes
  • Skillet cooking for grilled and fried foods
  • Knife mechanics for efficient food preparation
