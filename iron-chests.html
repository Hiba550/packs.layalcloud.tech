Iron Chests

Expands storage options with upgraded chests of various materials and sizes

Fabric/Forge Storage by ProgWML6

Description

Iron Chests is a simple yet essential storage expansion mod that adds upgraded chests made from various materials. Each chest type offers increased storage capacity, making inventory management much more efficient.

Chest Types

  • Iron Chest - 54 slots (double vanilla chest)
  • Gold Chest - 81 slots (1.5x iron chest)
  • Diamond Chest - 108 slots (2x iron chest)
  • Crystal Chest - 108 slots (transparent glass)
  • Obsidian Chest - 108 slots (explosion-resistant)
  • Copper Chest - 45 slots (budget upgrade)
  • Silver Chest - 72 slots (mid-tier option)

Features

  • Progressive upgrade system
  • Chest-to-chest upgrade without losing items
  • Beautiful textures for each material
  • Maintains vanilla chest functionality
  • Compatible with hoppers and comparators
  • Explosion-resistant obsidian variant
  • Transparent crystal chest for easy viewing

Upgrade System

  • Right-click with upgrade materials
  • Preserves all stored items during upgrade
  • Cost-effective storage expansion
  • Reversible upgrades (some variants)

Why Use Iron Chests

  • Essential for modded gameplay
  • Saves space in storage rooms
  • Beautiful and themed storage options
  • Time-tested and reliable
