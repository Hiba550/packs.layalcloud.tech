Description

Mekanism is the ultimate industrial technology mod, offering comprehensive machinery, advanced power generation, sophisticated ore processing systems, and cutting-edge chemical reactors. Transform your Minecraft world into a high-tech industrial paradise.

Key Features

  • Advanced machinery with 5x ore processing
  • Comprehensive power generation systems
  • Chemical and gas processing plants

  • Advanced transportation networks
  • Comprehensive tool and armor sets

  • Ore Processing: 2x, 3x, 4x, and 5x multiplication
  • Power Generation: Wind, solar, gas, and nuclear
  • Chemical Processing: Gas and liquid systems

  • Fusion Reactor: Ultimate power generation
  • Quantum Entangloporter: Instant transportation
