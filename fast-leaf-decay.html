Fast Leaf Decay

Makes leaves disappear instantly when trees are cut down

Fabric/Forge QoL by olafskiii

Description

Fast Leaf Decay is a simple yet essential quality of life mod that solves one of Minecraft's most annoying issues - floating leaves after cutting down trees. When you remove the last log from a tree, all connected leaves will instantly disappear, making forestry much cleaner and more efficient.

Features

  • Instant leaf decay when trees are cut down
  • No more floating leaf blocks cluttering your world
  • Works with all tree types and modded trees
  • Preserves natural leaf decay timing for player-placed leaves
  • Lightweight and performance-friendly
  • Configurable decay radius and timing
  • Compatible with tree-cutting mods
  • No impact on normal gameplay mechanics

Quality of Life Benefits

  • Cleaner world appearance after deforestation
  • No need to manually remove floating leaves
  • Faster and more efficient tree farming
  • Reduced visual clutter in cleared areas
  • Automatic sapling and stick drops from decaying leaves
  • Better performance in heavily forested areas

Configuration Options

  • Adjustable decay speed and radius
  • Toggle for different tree types
  • Whitelist/blacklist for specific leaf blocks
  • Compatibility settings for other mods
