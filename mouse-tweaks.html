Mouse Tweaks

Enhanced inventory management with intelligent mouse wheel support

Fabric/Forge QoL by YaLTeR

Description

Mouse Tweaks is an essential quality of life mod that revolutionizes inventory management in Minecraft. It adds intelligent mouse wheel scrolling and enhanced drag-and-drop functionality to make handling items faster, more intuitive, and significantly more efficient than vanilla Minecraft.

Features

  • Mouse wheel scrolling to transfer items between inventories
  • Drag items to automatically sort and distribute them
  • Right-click drag to distribute single items across slots
  • Shift-click dragging for mass item movement
  • Scroll wheel support in all inventory types
  • Compatible with modded inventories and containers
  • Highly configurable mouse behavior
  • No client-server desync issues

Mouse Wheel Features

  • Scroll up to move items to higher priority slots
  • Scroll down to move items to lower priority slots
  • Works in player inventory, chests, and crafting tables
  • Intelligent sorting based on item type and stack size
  • Respects container capacity and item limits
  • Smooth and responsive item movement

Drag and Drop Enhancements

  • Left-click drag to spread items evenly across slots
  • Right-click drag to place one item per slot
  • Shift-drag for mass item transfer
  • Smart item distribution based on existing stacks
  • Visual feedback during drag operations
