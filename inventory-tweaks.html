Inventory Tweaks

Smart inventory organization and sorting with customizable rules

Forge QoL by Kobata

Description

Inventory Tweaks is a legendary quality of life mod that has been helping Minecraft players organize their inventories for over a decade. It automatically sorts and organizes your inventory based on customizable rules, making item management effortless and keeping your storage clean and efficient.

Features

  • Automatic inventory sorting with customizable rules
  • One-click inventory organization
  • Auto-refill tools and items from inventory
  • Smart item replacement when tools break
  • Configurable sorting algorithms and priorities
  • Support for custom item categories
  • Chest and container sorting capabilities
  • Hotbar management and optimization

Sorting Features

  • Automatic item categorization and grouping
  • Customizable sorting rules and priorities
  • Support for modded items and containers
  • Intelligent hotbar arrangement
  • Stack optimization and consolidation
  • Preserve important item positions

Auto-Refill System

  • Automatically replace broken tools
  • Refill consumable items (food, arrows, blocks)
  • Smart item prioritization
  • Configurable refill behavior
  • Hotbar optimization and management

Configuration Options

  • Custom sorting rules and item categories
  • Adjustable sorting algorithms
  • Hotkey customization
  • Blacklist/whitelist for specific items
  • Server-side compatibility settings
